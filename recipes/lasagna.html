Lasagna Recipe

Designed by FreePik

Description

Easy and delicious lasagna recipe to make for the family to enjoy. Everyone will be fighting for seconds, It's that good! This recipe is for 12 servings

Ingredients

    • 1lb lean ground beef
      1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
      32 ounces cottage cheese
      3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
      2 eggs
      1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
      2 teaspoons dried parsley
      salt to taste
      ground black pepper to taste
      9 lasagna noodles
      1/2 cup water
